FEDERAL UNIVERSITY OF PETROLEUM RESOURCES EFFURUN
DELTA STATE

The Federal University of Petroleum Resources, Effurun (FUPRE) is an internationally recognized Nigerian university and is located in Delta State. It’s the first petroleum university in Africa and sixth in the world. Federal University of Petroleum Resources, Effurun has been officially accredited and/or recognized by the National Universities Commission (NUC), Nigeria.

The Federal University Of Petroleum Resources, consists of two colleges which are College of Science and College of Technology. These Colleges are sub divided into 18 departments, below are a list of departments in FUPRE
